Band History

One Direction (commonly abbreviated as 1D) is an English-Irish pop boy band formed in London in 2010. The group is composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and previously Zayn Malik until his departure from the group in 2015.

They became one of the best-selling boy bands of all time before going on an indefinite hiatus in 2016.
